
Subway Station06

1.0 - First version


This package contains Subway Station 06 and low poly props with scene for your game.

Mobile ready!

Pack includes Prefabs:

-Subway Station
-Subway Corridor
-Subway Train Body1
-Subway Train Body2
-Subway Train Doors
-Subway Tunnel - 2
-Ticket Machine
-Tourniquet
-Calumn - 3
-Bench
-Panel - 4

Features:
- Every object is prefabbed
- Scene with baked GI


Train Doors (left and right) are closed and positions are identical to the Subway Train Body (if you want to open doors)

Corridor columns positions are identical to the Subway Corridor


(Note - not modular, no animation)
